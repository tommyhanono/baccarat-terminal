Objective: Get closer to 9 than the other hand.

Card values: A=1 · 2–9=face · 10/J/Q/K=0 · total = sum mod 10

Player 3rd card: 0–5 draws · 6–7 stands · 8–9 Natural

Banker 3rd card:

0–2: always draw · 3: draw unless P3=8 · 4: draw if P3=2–7

5: draw if P3=4–7 · 6: draw if P3=6–7 · 7: stands · 8–9: Natural

If Player stood: Banker draws on 0–5, stands on 6–7.

Payouts: Player 1:1 · Banker 0.95:1 (5% comm) · Tie 8:1

Shoe: 8 decks, reshuffled when <15 cards remain.

Banker has the lowest house edge (~1.06%). Tie has the worst (~14.4%).
